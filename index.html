Stock Market Glossary (A-Z)

Our comprehensive glossary includes over 200 stock market terms explained in simple language with specific Nepali context. Here's a preview of terms from each letter:

A

Ask Price

The lowest price a seller is willing to accept for a stock.

Annual Report

Yearly document published by companies with financial statements and business activities.

Asset Allocation

Distribution of investments across different asset categories.

B

Bear Market

Market condition marked by falling prices and pessimism.

Bonus Shares

Free additional shares given to existing shareholders.

Book Value

Net asset value of a company divided by outstanding shares.

C

Capital Gain

Profit from selling a stock at a higher price than purchase price.

Circuit Breaker

Temporary halt in trading triggered by significant market movements.

Collateral

Assets pledged as security for a loan or other obligation.

D

DEMAT Account

Electronic account holding shares in digital format instead of physical certificates.

Dividend

Distribution of a company's earnings to shareholders.

Day Trading

Buying and selling securities within the same trading day.

E

Earnings Per Share (EPS)

Company's profit divided by outstanding shares.

EDIS

Electronic Delivery Instruction Slip for transferring shares.

Exchange

Marketplace where securities are bought and sold (e.g., NEPSE).

F

Face Value

Original value of a share as stated on the certificate.

Fundamental Analysis

Evaluating stocks based on financial and economic factors.

Floor Price

Minimum price at which a stock can be traded on a given day.

The complete glossary in the book covers all letters A-Z with detailed explanations of over 200 terms.
